Frequently Asked Questions

How do I connect WhatsApp API?

To connect WhatsApp API, go to Settings > Integration Settings and click "Connect" next to WhatsApp API. You'll need to register for a WhatsApp Business API account and obtain your API credentials. Enter the credentials in the popup window and click "Verify" to complete the connection.

Why aren't my reminders being sent?

Check the following: 1) Make sure reminders are enabled in Reminders settings. 2) Verify your WhatsApp/Email integrations are connected. 3) Ensure patient phone numbers are correctly formatted with country codes.

How do I export my data?

Go to the Leads page and click the "Export" button. You can choose to export all leads or selected leads. The system will generate a CSV file that you can download.

What do the lead status colors mean?

New (Purple): Recently added leads. Contacted (Blue): Initial contact made. Qualified (Green): Confirmed interest. Booked (Orange): Appointment scheduled. Completed (Green): Treatment done. Lost (Red): Did not convert.

How do I set up auto-routing rules?

Go to the Qualification page and scroll to "Auto-Routing Rules". Click "Add Rule" and define your conditions (budget, urgency, source) and the corresponding action (High Value tag, Priority Booking, etc.).

Common Errors & Solutions

WhatsApp Message Failed
Solution: Check if the phone number includes country code. Ensure your message template is pre-approved by WhatsApp. Try using the fallback SMS option.
Calendar Sync Error
Solution: Reconnect Google Calendar in Settings > Integrations. Enable 2-way sync in your Google Calendar settings. Add buffer time between appointments.
Duplicate Lead Entries
Solution: Enable deduplication rules in Settings. Search for existing contacts before adding new leads. Use unique identifiers like phone number or email.
Lead Not Receiving Messages
Solution: Add 3-step follow-up sequence. Offer call-back option. Check if the phone number is valid. Verify message template approval status.
